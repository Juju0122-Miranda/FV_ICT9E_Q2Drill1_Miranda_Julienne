Overview
Emergency Go Bags are a requirement in every household--it contains the neccessary items needed for the human body to do work. It is essential for our survival as it contains important needs such as food and water, medical kits, and etc..Having an emergency go bag on stand-by reduces panic and will allow for us to smoothly exit the area and evacuate safely.

Medical Kit

When out of reach with hospitals or evacuation centers, medical kits will serve as your first aid. It is so that minor injuries can be temporarily treated until professional help arrives. In addition to that, medical kits should also contain over the counter medicines and prescribed medicines.

Food and Water

In a scenario where food is scare in your evacuation centers, the bag must contain some light and heavy snacks that will prevent hunger and weakness. This ensures that you have the energy and strength to remain focus and alert at all times.

Important Documents

Essential for rebuilding, proving ownership, and identity in case of emergency after the calamity.

  • Birth certificates
  • Bank statements
  • Land titles
  • Passports
  • Valid ids

Clothes

Extra clothes are essential for extended stays at evacuation centers to provide some sort of comfort.

  • Sleeved shirts
  • Long sleeves
  • Jogging pants
  • Leggings
  • Shorts
  • Socks
